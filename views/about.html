Our Story

Our Story

Where Heritage Meets
Luxury.

Mangal Prime was born from a simple conviction: the finest Turkish dining in the Greater Toronto Area need not live only in the heart of the city. It began with Mangal Kebab House—a place that earned the trust of Scarborough and beyond with authentic flavours, generous hospitality, and a commitment to quality that never wavered.

That same soul now finds a new expression in Richmond Hill. Mangal Prime is not a copy of the original; it is its evolution. Here, the recipes and traditions we hold dear are elevated—premium ingredients, masterful technique, and presentation worthy of the most discerning guest. Valet at the door. A table reserved in your name. The glow of the grill and the warmth of Turkish welcome, refined for an evening you will remember.

We honour the richness of Turkish cuisine—the smoke of the mangal, the silk of yogurt and brown butter, the crackle of fresh lavash and the depth of spices passed down through generations. Every dish tells a story of place and craft. Every visit is an invitation to slow down, savour, and be cared for. This is fine dining in Toronto’s north: authentic, luxurious, and unmistakably Mangal.
