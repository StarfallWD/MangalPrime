Where Heritage Meets Luxury.

Mangal Prime was born from a simple conviction: the finest Turkish dining in the Greater Toronto Area need not live only in the heart of the city. It began with Mangal Kebab House—a place that earned the trust of Scarborough and beyond with authentic flavours, generous hospitality, and a commitment to quality that never wavered.

That same soul now finds a new expression in Richmond Hill. We honour the richness of Turkish cuisine—the smoke of the mangal, the silk of yogurt and brown butter, the crackle of fresh lavash. Every visit is an invitation to slow down, savour, and be cared for. This is fine dining in Toronto's north: authentic, luxurious, and unmistakably Mangal.

Our Story

Luxury Turkish Dining,
Reimagined.

Mangal Prime is the second chapter of the Mangal story—a bold evolution from our beloved roots into Richmond Hill’s most refined Turkish restaurant. Here, authentic Turkish cuisine meets fine-dining craftsmanship: premium ingredients, meticulous presentation, and service that anticipates every need.

We believe the highest level of Turkish dining in the GTA doesn’t have to live only downtown. Valet-serviced, reservation-driven, and designed for memorable evenings—Mangal Prime invites you to discover what happens when heritage meets luxury.
